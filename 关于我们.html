LY.COM

LY.com is China's leading provider for leisure travel. It was founded in 2004 and is headquatered in Suzhou, China. LY.com's rapid growth and innovative business models have been widely acknowledged by the industry. In 2014, it abtained more than RMB 2 billion in investment from Tencent and Ctrip. In July, 2015, LY.com obtained more than RMB 6 billion in stratergic investment from Wanda, Tencent and CITIC Capital.


LY.com is a state-level high-tech enterprise, as well as aong the first group of E-commence demonstration enterprises authorized b the Ministry of Commence. It has been voted as one of the "Top 20 Tourism Groups in China" for several years in a row, and is one of the five largest business groups in the travel industry in China. For the new decade, the company has set the strategic goal of becoming NO. 1 in leisure travel. Currently, the company enjoys leading positions in the admission ticket booking market for attractions in China and in cruise travel. It is also making forward-moving deployments in areas such as overseas travel, domestic travel and nearby travel.


LY.COM provided service to more than 300 million people in China in 2016. Helping more people to enjoy the fun of traveling is the mission of LY.COM.
