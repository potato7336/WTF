Refund Policy

1. Ticket refund online service hour from 06:00 to 23:00, and at least 40 minutes prior to departure

2. Ticket refund handling fee is charged according to the time prior to departure:

①15+ days prior to departure: free

②48hours - 15days prior to departure: 5% of ticket price

③24-48hours prior to departure: 10% of ticket price

④within 24hours before departure: 20% of ticket price


3. Under any of the following circumstances, please go to the railway station's refund window with a valid ID for ticket refund service:

①You have picked up your print-out ticket

②Out of online service hours (between 23:00-6:00)

③Within 40 minutes to departure

4. If you need a receipt for your ticket refund handling fee, please go to  the railway station's refund window with a valid ID and your ticket pickup number, which is the letter E followed by a series of numbers. Please ask for the receipt within 10days since your ticket refund.
